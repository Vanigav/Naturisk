06 : Anthracnose

Information

Anthracnose is a fungal disease of shade trees caused by Colletotrichum lagenarium, which results in leaf spots. and the cupping and curling of leaves. Sever infection can result in disconcerting levels of premature leaf drop and defoliation.

What to look for

Irregular tan, brown, or papery spots will develop along the margins or veins of the leaves. Completely dead spots on leaves are also common.

How to stop it?

Common fungicides can prevent Anthracnose from appearing on trees and from spreading further, and the disease can usually go away upon a warm or dry season again. Most trees will recover, and in most cases, it is not fatal.
