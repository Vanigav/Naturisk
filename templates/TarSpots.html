02 : Tar Spots

Information

Tar spot is a type of fungi that forms in damp places, such as leaf litter, and are caused by spores, which can lead to infections.

What to look for

Initial symptoms of your tree having tar spot are small yellow spots and patches on your leaves. Over time, these spots will develop into black patches, sometimes clustered together.

How to stop it?

For most maple trees and shrubs, tar spot is a very harmless disease! tar spot cannot kill any of your plants, but it can alter their appearance.
